Understanding Diabetes

What is Diabetes?

Diabetes is a chronic condition that affects how your body processes blood sugar (glucose). Type 1 diabetes results from the body’s inability to produce insulin, while Type 2 diabetes involves insulin resistance or insufficient insulin production.

Symptoms

  • Increased thirst and frequent urination
  • Fatigue and blurred vision
  • Slow-healing sores or frequent infections
  • Unexplained weight loss (Type 1)

Management and Lifestyle

At NSG Fitness, our programs include regular monitoring of blood sugar levels, a balanced diet rich in fiber and low in processed sugars, and regular physical activity and exercise plans (e.g., 30-45 minute sessions of cardio and strength training) and nutrition guidance to support blood sugar control and overall health.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can exercise help manage diabetes?

Yes, regular exercise improves insulin sensitivity and helps regulate blood sugar levels.

Do I need to follow a strict diet?

A balanced diet with controlled portions is key, but our plans are flexible to suit your needs.

How often should I check my blood sugar?

This depends on your condition and doctor’s advice, often 1-4 times daily for Type 1 or as recommended for Type 2.
